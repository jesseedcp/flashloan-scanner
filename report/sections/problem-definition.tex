\section{Problem Definition and Scope}

This project focuses on understanding and identifying flash loan transactions on Ethereum. The main research questions are: how flash loan providers and users interact in one transaction, how such transactions can be identified from on-chain data, and what their major applications look like in the DeFi ecosystem.

To keep the project feasible, we limit the analysis to Ethereum mainnet and selected representative protocols. We focus on rule-based identification rather than machine learning, because protocol-aware rules are easier to explain, verify, and evaluate within the time constraints of the course project.
