\section{Abstract}

This report studies flash loans and their applications in the DeFi ecosystem. The project focuses on three tasks: explaining how flash-loan providers and users interact within one atomic transaction, designing protocol-aware patterns to identify flash-loan-like transactions, and building a validation-oriented backend workflow over Ethereum mainnet samples. We limit the scope to Ethereum mainnet and three representative protocol families, namely Aave V3, Balancer V2, and Uniswap V2, and we adopt a rule-based detection approach supported by event-backed and trace-backed verification. The final outcome is a reproducible prototype scanner, exported validation results from a real-mainnet evaluation window, and a presentation-ready summary of the system design and findings.
