\section{Abstract}

This report studies flash loans and their applications in the DeFi ecosystem. Our project focuses on three tasks: explaining the interaction process between flash loan providers and users, designing patterns to identify flash loan transactions, and collecting and analyzing flash loan transactions from Ethereum. We limit the project scope to Ethereum mainnet and selected representative protocols, and we adopt a rule-based detection approach supported by transaction-level verification. The final outcome of the project includes a reproducible implementation, a cleaned dataset, statistical analysis, representative case studies, and a presentation-ready summary of findings.
